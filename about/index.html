About Me

Hello! I’m Kunal Kushwaha, a technical architect with over 20 years of experience building system‑level products and cloud‑native solutions. My core passions lie at the intersection of AI and cloud technologies, where I design scalable architectures, leverage container ecosystems, and integrate machine learning workflows to solve complex challenges.

What I Do

  • AI & Cloud Architecture I architect and deploy AI‑driven applications on public and private clouds, harnessing tools like Kubernetes, Docker, and serverless frameworks to deliver robust, high‑availability systems.
  • Container & Infrastructure Engineering With 8+ years in container technologies (LXC, Docker, Kubernetes) and nearly 7 years in Linux kernel and embedded development, I streamline CI/CD pipelines, optimize resource utilization, and contribute to open‑source runtimes such as Podman and Containerd.
  • Developer Ecosystems & Community I love empowering teams through hands‑on training, public speaking, and building vibrant communities. I’ve organized meetups, delivered workshops on AI/ML integration, and written tutorials to help engineers adopt best practices in cloud‑native development.

Professional Journey

Over two decades, I’ve:

  • Led global R&D teams in designing RAID firmwares, storage drivers, and data‑pipeline tools.
  • Partnered with enterprise customers to tailor AI/ML solutions, deploy analytics platforms, and implement secure identity integrations.
  • Contributed code and design reviews to major open‑source projects, fostering collaboration and innovation.

Outside Tech

When I’m not architecting cloud solutions or experimenting with new AI frameworks, I pursue photography and filmmaking. Capturing stories through my lens keeps my creativity sharp—and you can follow my visual journeys on Instagram.

Feel free to explore my work on GitHub or connect on LinkedIn. I’m always eager to discuss AI innovations, cloud strategies, or creative media collaborations.
